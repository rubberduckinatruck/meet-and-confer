District finance explorer

Where the financial conversation starts

The current site explains Arizona school finance concepts such as M&O, RCL, DAA, ADM, Classroom Site Fund, carryforward, overrides, bonds, and UCO. This redesign turns those definitions into a guided finance explorer for employees.

Key distinction: employees need to see whether dollars are recurring, one-time, restricted, or unrestricted before judging whether a proposal is sustainable.

Compensation center

Salary, stipend, and market comparison hub

The current site already tracks compensation changes since RedForEd and compares certified, classified, administrative, and extra duty compensation. This redesign makes that information searchable and action-oriented.

Certified Staff

Benchmarks: Bachelor’s Year 0, Bachelor’s Year 5, Master’s Year 0, Master’s Year 5. Include 301, performance pay, and Instructional Improvement funds when available.

Classified Staff

Track starting pay, wage growth, competitive position, performance pay, holiday compensation, and hard-to-fill role comparisons.

Administration

Use starting salary comparisons because schedules and credit for experience vary across districts. Include stipends and performance pay when available.

Raise impact calculator

Local estimate only. Enter a current salary and proposed recurring increase.
